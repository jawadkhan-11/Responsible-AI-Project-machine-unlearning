HOW THE UNLEARNING WORKS
============================================================

When you paste a prompt into the dashboard playground, the
system scans your text for "anchor terms" — words or phrases
uniquely associated with Harry Potter — and replaces each
with a generic counterpart from the unlearning dictionary.

Examples of replacements:
  Hogwarts       →  Mystic Academy
  Harry          →  Jon
  Ron            →  Tom
  Hermione       →  Emma
  Quidditch      →  Skyball
  Voldemort      →  The Dark One
  Dumbledore     →  The Headmaster
  Slytherin      →  Serpent House
  Gryffindor     →  Lion House
  Felix Felicis  →  Fortune Elixir
  Horcrux        →  Soul Fragment
  Marauder's Map →  Explorer's Chart
  Diagon Alley   →  Magic Market
  Hogwarts Express → Academy Train

The dashboard also shows:
  • A familiarity score BEFORE unlearning
  • A familiarity score AFTER unlearning (target: ≤ 0.01)
  • A badge list of every anchor term that was replaced
